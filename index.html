My favourite anime's

ONE PIECE

The series focuses on Monkey D. Luffy, a young man made of rubber, who is inspired by his childhood idol, the powerful pirate Shanks. So he sets off on a journey from the East Blue Sea to find the mythical treasure, the One Piece.

NARUTO

The series focuses on Uzumaki Naruto, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage (the leader of a village). But he has sealed the Nine-Tails demon inside him and the villagers are scared of him, so his life is really hard.

BLEACH

The series focuses on Kurosaki Ichigo, a young man who suddenly gets powers of "Soul Reaper" from Rukia Kuchiki who had lost her Soul Reaper powers while she was protecting Ichigo's family. That's how his Soul Reaper journey started.

DEMON SLAYER

The series focuses on Kamado Tanjiro, a young man who wants to become a demon slayer and defeat Muzan Kibutsuji after his family was slaughtered by demon and his younger sister, Nezuko, turned into a demon.
